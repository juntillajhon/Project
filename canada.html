HOME TO THE ROCKIES: CANADA

    Step into the world’s second-biggest country that is a unique mix of raw beauty and majestic landscapes. Canada’s spectacular scenery ranges from serene lakes to lofty mountains, the gorgeous Rocky mountain range, also home to polar bears and caribou, as well as glaciers and fjords. One of the most beautiful countries in the world, Canada is also one of the coolest one-stop destinations for family vacations and hikers. Come here to experience the surreal Aurora Borealis, the UNESCO World Heritage Site of the Canadian Rockies, Yukon territory’s great wilderness, and B.C. mountain slopes that draw skiers and snowboarders.
