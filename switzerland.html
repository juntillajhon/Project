PARADISE ON EARTH: SWITZERLAND

    With its breathtaking scenery that ranges from astonishing mountain ranges, lakes, landscapes with no horizon, waterfalls, Switzerland isn’t a surprise entry on this list of the most beautiful countries in the world. It has some spectacular spaces like Lauterbrunnen, with its 72 thundering waterfalls, the four-armed Lake Lucerne, St-Léonard lake (Europe’s largest underground lake), the majestic Matterhorn, the world’s most famous tooth-shaped mountain peak. You can visit the quaint lakeside towns of Vevey, Montreux and Luzern, and contrast it with Geneva’s aristocratic gravitas, and the tranquil villages of Zermatt and the very urban and cosmopolitan Zurich. Tucked away in the Alps, this country is an absolute must-visit destination and usually features on everyone’s bucket list due to its breathtaking natural beauty.
